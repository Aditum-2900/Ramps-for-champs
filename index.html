Who are we?

Aditum is a company that caters to the needs of individuals whose demands may not be apparent to the public. We provide products to improve the quality of life of indivuals who have been denied of many privileges due to special conditions. We have noticed that many of the existing solutions either are inneficient or extremely out of reach for the vast majority. Therefore, we aim to create a better environment for these challenged individuals through our innovative products.

Why do we exist?

We have seen many changes in the current special aid market, but unfortunately, none of those changes addressed the fundemental problems that are outdated technologies being still used and treated as normal
Therefore, we decided to create our own innovative and creative solutions in order to ensure that no one is left behind

What do we do?

Aditum designs and sells tools for physically impaired people to allow them to have greater mobility within the city. We created a specialized wheelchairs that supports a motorized extendable ramp system that can be used to go over everyday obstacles such as stairs
