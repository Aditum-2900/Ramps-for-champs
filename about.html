About Aditum

Faciliate Accesibility Worldwide

We are a team of five striving to solve daily problems to improve the lives of those whose's needs are often overlooked. Globally, there are approximately 65 million people in the world who use wheelchairs daily. The creation of affordable wheelchairs with portable ramps allows the handicapped population to access the many places that aren't wheelchair accessible.
